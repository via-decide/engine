Cashback Rules

Last updated: Feb 2026 • Cashback is funded only from eligible affiliate/bounty earnings.
STRICT No proof = no payout.
Cashback is paid only after you submit valid purchase proof and we approve your claim.

1) Who can claim cashback?

  • You must be a registered member (₹1/month).
  • You must have used our deal link (affiliate tag on the clickout).
  • You must submit purchase proof within 7 days of delivery.

2) What proof is required?

  • Order proof: screenshot of Amazon “Order Details” page OR invoice screenshot.
  • Product proof: product name visible (or item page inside order).
  • Amount proof: total paid amount visible.
  • Payout proof: your UPI ID for payout.
If any of the above is missing/blurred, the claim can be rejected.

3) Exclusions (zero cashback)

  • Excluded products: Flight bookings, Gift Cards, Video gaming consoles & hardware, Prime membership, Kindle eBooks, Gold & Silver coins.
  • 0% advertising fee products (example): many iPhone models and listed phone/device SKUs → cashback may be ₹0.
If Amazon pays us 0%, we cannot pay cashback. We will show this clearly on approval.

4) Cashback calculation (max but safe)

Cashback is based on the category advertising fee rate. We pay an aggressive share but keep a holdback for reversals and fraud.

  • Cashback rate = 70% of the category fee rate (unless capped).
  • Per-order cap: we may cap cashback for very high-value items.
  • Holdback: 30% retained to protect against affiliate reversals.

5) Bounty cashback (Prime / Audible / Amazon Business)

If you complete an eligible bounty event, cashback is a fixed amount:

  • ₹100 bounty → ₹60 cashback
  • ₹150 bounty → ₹90 cashback
  • ₹200 bounty → ₹120 cashback
  • ₹400 bounty → ₹240 cashback
Bounty eligibility depends on Amazon’s rules and can be reversed.

6) Approval, payout timeline & refunds

  • Review SLA: 24–72 hours after you submit a valid claim.
  • Payout cadence: weekly batch payouts.
  • Refunds/returns: if you return/cancel, cashback becomes invalid and may be reversed.

7) Fraud policy

  • Fake/edited screenshots → permanent ban.
  • Repeated invalid claims → account blocked.
We keep logs to protect the program for genuine users.
