Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 2026

Overview

Decide ("we", "our", "us") operates as a demo marketplace platform. This privacy policy explains how we handle your information.

Information We Collect

  • Waitlist Data: Email, phone (optional), city, interests
  • Feedback: Your messages, ratings, and suggestions
  • Seller Interest: Business details, contact information
  • Technical Data: Device ID (browser-generated), session data
  • Demo Data: Cart, orders, preferences (stored locally in your browser)

How We Use Your Information

  • To send you updates about Decide launch and features
  • To improve our product based on your feedback
  • To contact sellers who express interest
  • To provide customer support
  • Analytics and platform improvement

Data Storage

Server-side: Waitlist, feedback, and seller interest are stored securely in Supabase (PostgreSQL database with encryption at rest).

Client-side: Demo data (cart, orders, UTR) is stored locally in your browser using localStorage. This data never leaves your device unless you explicitly submit it (e.g., feedback form).

Data Sharing

We do NOT sell your data. We may share information with:

  • Service Providers: Supabase (database), Cloudflare (hosting)
  • Legal Requirements: If required by law

Your Rights

  • Access: Request a copy of your data
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your data
  • Correction: Update your information
  • Opt-out: Unsubscribe from emails anytime

Data Retention

  • Waitlist: Until product launch + 90 days
  • Feedback: 2 years
  • Seller Interest: Until onboarding complete or 1 year
  • Demo Data: Stored in your browser until you clear it

Security

We use industry-standard security measures:

  • HTTPS encryption for all data transmission
  • Database encryption at rest (Supabase)
  • Row-level security policies
  • No payment data stored (demo UPI IDs only)

Cookies

We use minimal cookies and browser storage for:

  • Device ID generation (for demo tracking)
  • Session management
  • Demo cart and order data

Children's Privacy

Decide is not intended for users under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy. Changes will be posted on this page with updated date.
